TəqvimPro Privacy Policy

TəqvimPro does not collect, store on servers, or share any personal data.

  • Location: Location information is used only on your device to calculate prayer times and the qibla direction. It is never transmitted to us or to third parties.
  • Exchange rates: Exchange rates are fetched from the Central Bank of Azerbaijan (cbar.az) and CoinGecko without sending any personal information.
  • Events, reminders and settings: Events, reminders and application settings are stored only on your device.
  • Notifications: Notifications, including adhan, holidays and reminders, are scheduled locally on your device.
